  Cloud computing

  Cloud computing has become a popular technology and it has been used all around the world and especially large companies like Google, Facebook, Microsoft, etc. So, what does it really do?

  Cloud computing is a technology that provides computing resources for the users through the Internet. These resources are anything related to computing, it can be a software, service, data, … and they will be stored on the Internet. Users can connect to any of these resources on cloud, at anytime and anywhere, as long as they have Internet connection.

  Before cloud computing was invented, when we wanted to do something, we had to spend a lot of investments from beginning to the end. For personal use, if we wanted to store data, we had to buy a hard drive. If we wanted to manage your store business, we had to buy our own management software and download it on our computers. Not only that, but we also had to spend money on maintenance. For example, if the hard drive is broken, we have to replace or fix it. Besides, in order to avoid the risks of losing data, we have to back up our data frequently in another drive, which make it really inconvenient.

  That is for personal use, but for large company like Microsoft, the maintenance fees are a lot more because their management system is really big and complicated, with data in and out continuously, so that is why only a small error can make them lose a ton of millions dollar. They will also have to spend a lot of money on staffs in order to check, test, run and update their system.

  Then cloud computing has been invented, and it has solved many problems. Whenever we want to back up our files, there are OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive to help us. We do not have to use our own hard drives and maintain anymore, because the companies who provide cloud computing have already done that. If the hard drive is broken, they will automatically replace it and backup the data. For the companies, they have moved their apps and software into cloud. The users only need to open the browser, click and then they can access to the software. They do not have to have to download on their computer anymore.

  These are the most common benefits of cloud computing:

  • The biggest benefit is money and time saving.
  • Cloud computing is very convenient, as long as we have access to the Internet.
  • Cloud computing also provide data safety and continuity
  Cloud computing has become the key factor influencing promoting the business process of many companies, and help these companies work more fluently

  On the other hand, it also has some potential problems that we must deal with. Nowadays, in Vietnam, cloud computing has continued to grow a lot but there are still some drawbacks:
  • First, every company has different scale, organization model and different fields. That is why when these companies switch to this technology, which is cloud computing, they will have to face some big challenges like capital source, investment costs, production costs, management, etc.
  • Second, cloud computing relies a lot on bandwidth speed, which will impact direct to the connection of the users, causing them to lose connection.
  • Finally, is about the cybersecurity problem. Every company must find a way in order to protect the important information from threats and hackers.
  Cloud computing also leaves a lot of impacts around us. Cloud computing has become a very popular technology and it has been focused by lots of companies around the world. Generally, cloud computing has replaced hard drives because many users have started to use cloud computing to access software and store their data. So, for software employees and engineers, they will need getting use to cloud computing and gain knowledges about this technology so that they can make the most of it. In Vietnam, this technology is still a vague for lots of people, they only stop at researching this but not getting used to it. That is why in order to make the work more effective, Vietnam must start developing skills about cloud computing.

  In my daily life, cloud computing has affected me a lot. With cloud computing, I do not have to worry about buying a hard drive to store and back up my files anymore. It is very useful to recover files after any accidents and it is very fast and convenient. Communicating with my friends and families has become a lot easier thanks to cloud computing, now I can access to any software as long as I have access to the Internet. Another thing is that I can also store telephone numbers on cloud. Whenever I change my phone, I have to reinstall my telephone numbers which takes a lot of times. Thanks to cloud, whenever I add a new phone number, it will automatically update on cloud.

  Cybersecurity and privacy

  All the organizations have to face a lot of threats that can cause data stolen and attacks. In order to prevent these from happening, understanding about cybersecurity and privacy is the key factor to solve this problem.

  All the organizations have to face a lot of threats that can cause data stolen and attacks. In order to prevent these from happening, understanding about cybersecurity and privacy is the key factor to solve this problem.

  Nowadays, cybersecurity is the most important objective that every company and organization must focus on. In order to gain the trust from customer and keep their information safe, they must minimize the risk and fix the error. And the only one who can fix the errors is human.

  In recent years, there has been a number of webpages being attacked by hackers, and a thousand of computer being infected by virus, especially in Vietnam. The purpose of these attacks are data and money stolen, taking over control, changing and modifying data, posting fake news, etc. The reasons responsible for this are variable:
  • Due to the rapid development of information technology, internet has provided chances and motivations to develop social and economic, but it also provides challenges. For example, there will some gaps in new technology which can be bypassed and attacked by hackers, causing them to break the laws.
  • Another reason is that cybersecurity and privacy protection have not been given enough adequate attention.
  • What is more, because of this rapid development, we have to keep on investing money on new technology, which makes it very difficult for developing country like Vietnam.
  Despite of these challenges and difficulties, there are something that can be done now:
  • In order to fight against threats and hackers, one of the most important solutions is human resources. IT professionals and staffs play a vital role in preventing cybercrimes, that is why company must provide policy for them in order to gain knowledges about new technology and promote all their professional capabilities.
  • Government and companies also must strengthen data protection, research and find suitable methods to prevent cybercrimes.
  Also, every person can follow these steps to protect ourselves from cybercrimes:
  • Updating software and operating system so that these will install the latest security patches.
  • We can also use anti-virus software to detect and remove threats from our devices.
  • Whenever we set up passwords, we must use strong passwords to make sure that these passwords cannot be easily guessed.
  • Avoid emails or phone calls from unknown contacts, avoid accessing to strange websites.
  • Finally, we should limit using Wi-Fi in public places.
  In my daily life, cybersecurity has helped me a lot. Thanks to this technology, if my data is stored on the Internet, it will be protected by cybersecurity. What is more, cybersecurity gives me an opportunity to gain more knowledges about information technology, how data is stored and protected, how these prevent crimes and threats on the internet.
